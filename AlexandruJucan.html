Meet Drake, the fish!

The buttons

"KILL Drake" button will kill the fish and it will flow to the surface of the water

"Say Hello!" button will animate the fish mouth and he will say hello"

"SWIM" button will make the fish swim to the bottom of the aquarium and then to the left, using the inner fin

"Clear" button will reset the fish initial position

The canvas clicks

Click on the fish eye to make it blink.

Click on the fish body to enrage him for 1 second.

Click anywhere else on the canvas to teleport the fish randomly on the canvas.
